Marie is a sustainable finance strategist and researcher who helps organizations allocate and deploy capital to build a more respectful and inclusive future.

Her interest in systemic change deepened during her Master's degree in Sustainable Development, where she was introduced to the concept of degrowth and came to see the potential for reimagining our economic and social paradigms.

Guided by a strong commitment to care, equity, and collective well-being, Marie envisions a society where collaboration replaces competition and where shared values drive meaningful progress.

She is also passionate about bringing people together through thoughtfully designed events and can support your organization in creating safe and welcoming spaces for meaningful sharing.
