Matt Tutt works as a digital marketing consultant (external link) primarily for purpose-driven organisations and those people that are trying to "do good" for the environment or society. 

Based in Galicia, Spain, he is a huge fan of postgrowth and degrowth movements.

He created PostGrowth.co to try and bring other degrowthers together, where we can work as a collective to support other postgrowth movements on a pro-bono basis.  

He tries to spend as much time as he can supporting other groups in the degrowth, decolonising and delinking spaces, often with website support services.

He has also started a tech movement called DeGoogle (external link) - an attempt to raise awareness of the harms of Big Tech, but also to collectively encourage people to begin the process of "DeGoogling".
